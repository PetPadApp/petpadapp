Received a Shared Pet Profile?

To view the shared pet profile, follow these steps:

1
Download the App

Install Pet Pad from the App Store using the button below or by scanning the QR code.

2
Check Your Email

Look for an email containing the access PIN for the shared pet profile.

3
Open the Shared Link Again

After installing the app, return to the shared link and tap it to open in the Pet Pad app.

4
Enter the PIN

When prompted, enter the PIN from your email to access the shared pet profile.
