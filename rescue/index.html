Everything You Need to Save More Lives

Pet Pad gives fosters and shelters the tools to create rich pet profiles, work together, and connect adoptable pets with loving families.

Complete Pet Profiles

Build detailed profiles for every animal in your care. Store microchip numbers, vaccination history, medical records, feeding instructions, behavioral notes, emergency contacts, and more — all in one organized, accessible place.

Co-Manage with Your Team

Invite fosters, volunteers, and staff as coparents to collaborate on pet profiles in real time. Everyone stays on the same page with instant updates and shared access to critical information.

Post to the Adoption Website Instantly

Upgrade to the Rescue tier and toggle any pet as adoptable. They appear automatically on the Pet Pad Adoption website within moments. No waiting, no manual uploads, no back-and-forth.

Transfer to New Owners Seamlessly

When a pet finds their forever home, transfer the entire profile to the new parent with a single tap. All records, photos, and history move intact. The new owner gets full control and editability instantly.

How It Works

  • Download Pet Pad and upgrade to the Rescue tier ($29.99/year)
  • Create pet profiles with all important info, photos, and medical records
  • Invite coparents so your team can help manage and update profiles
  • Toggle "Mark as Adoptable" — pets appear instantly on our adoption website
  • Transfer the profile to the new owner when the perfect match is found
