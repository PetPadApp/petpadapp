Secure Pet Profile Sharing

No more writing lengthy pet care instructions or worrying about missing important details. Pet Pad's secure sharing feature lets you give pet sitters exactly what they need:

  • Create your pet's complete profile with medical history, emergency contacts, and feeding schedules
  • Generate a secure 'Share Link' protected by a 4-digit PIN
  • Share the link and PIN with your pet sitter
  • Sitters get instant read-only access to all care instructions — no account needed

Your pet's sensitive information stays protected with PIN verification and time-limited access.
